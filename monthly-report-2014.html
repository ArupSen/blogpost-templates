  • Audax

    No audax rides at all in September. I was so into it in July especially after my first DIY ride but already in August I had lost some of that impetus. I was very concerned about losing the fitness level that I had developed but the more I regained my enthusiasm of programming and computing the less concerned I became about riding. Now I'm a little concerned that I'm not concerned especially as my ethusiasm for riding was so high only a few weeks ago but there you have it, emotions are fickle: you think you want something so badly that you would give anything to get it and before you know it you don't value it at all. I'm not saying that I no longer value cycling any more, far from it but my enthusiasm certainly has dropped tremendously.
